# Prompt Formula Guide

Master the art of crafting effective grok imagine prompts with proven formulas and templates.

## The Universal Formula

```
[Subject] + [Action/Pose] + [Environment] + [Lighting] + [Style] + [Technical] + [Quality]
```

### Breaking Down Each Component

#### 1. Subject (What)
The main focus of your image.

**Examples:**
- "A mystical forest guardian"
- "Professional businesswoman"
- "Cyberpunk street vendor"
- "Ancient dragon"

#### 2. Action/Pose (Doing What)
What the subject is doing or how they're positioned.

**Examples:**
- "standing confidently"
- "mid-leap through air"
- "sitting contemplatively"
- "battling enemies"

#### 3. Environment (Where)
The setting and background context.

**Examples:**
- "in enchanted forest"
- "on city rooftop at night"
- "inside futuristic laboratory"
- "against white studio background"

#### 4. Lighting (How Lit)
Critical for mood and atmosphere.

**Examples:**
- "golden hour sunlight"
- "dramatic studio lighting"
- "neon city lights"
- "soft natural window light"

#### 5. Style (Aesthetic)
The artistic approach and medium.

**Examples:**
- "digital art"
- "oil painting style"
- "anime aesthetic"
- "photorealistic"

#### 6. Technical (Camera/Render)
Photography or rendering details.

**Examples:**
- "85mm lens"
- "shallow depth of field"
- "octane render"
- "f/1.4 aperture"

#### 7. Quality (Resolution/Detail)
Quality and detail keywords.

**Examples:**
- "8k uhd"
- "hyperdetailed"
- "professional photography"
- "trending on artstation"

## Modifier Stacking Strategies

### The "More is More" Approach
Stack related modifiers for emphasis:
```
Sharp focus, tack sharp, crystal clear, 8k uhd,
hyperdetailed, intricate details, highly detailed
```

### The "Balanced" Approach
One modifier per category:
```
8k uhd + photorealistic + sharp focus +
cinematic lighting + professional
```

### The "Minimalist" Approach
Essential keywords only:
```
8k uhd, photorealistic, dramatic lighting
```

## Advanced Techniques

### Contrast Technique
Specify what you want AND what you don't:
```
Sharp focus foreground, soft bokeh background
Detailed character, blurred environment
```

### Layer Technique
Build image in layers:
```
[Foreground element] + [middle ground element] +
[background element] + [atmospheric effects]
```

### Reference Stacking
Combine multiple style references:
```
Studio Ghibli meets Hayao Miyazaki meets anime aesthetic,
watercolor painting technique with digital art finish
```

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Problem: Image Too Generic

**Solution:**
- Add specific details to subject
- Include unique environmental elements
- Reference specific artists or styles
- Add distinctive lighting or atmosphere

**Before:** "Fantasy character"
**After:** "Half-elf ranger with glowing emerald eyes and intricate nature tattoos, wearing leather armor with silver filigree"

### Problem: Wrong Mood/Atmosphere

**Solution:**
- Specify lighting more precisely
- Add mood keywords (serene, ominous, cheerful)
- Include atmospheric effects (mist, rain, dust particles)
- Reference specific color palettes

**Before:** "Forest scene"
**After:** "Misty forest at dawn, soft ethereal lighting, peaceful atmosphere, muted greens and blues"

### Problem: Compositionally Weak

**Solution:**
- Specify composition rules (rule of thirds, golden ratio)
- Define foreground/background
- Add leading lines or framing
- Specify camera angle (low angle, bird's eye, etc.)

**Before:** "Mountain landscape"
**After:** "Mountain landscape with winding river as leading line, rule of thirds composition, foreground wildflowers, bird's eye view"

## Testing Your Formulas

**Method:**
1. Start with basic formula
2. Generate image
3. Identify what's missing or wrong
4. Add specific modifiers to address issues
5. Regenerate and compare
6. Refine until satisfied

**Example Iteration:**

**v1:** "Dragon"
**v2:** "Red dragon breathing fire"
**v3:** "Massive red dragon breathing fire in mountain lair"
**v4:** "Massive red dragon breathing fire in mountain lair, dramatic lighting, treasure hoard visible, fantasy digital art"
**v5:** "Massive red dragon breathing fire in mountain lair, dramatic lighting with glowing lava, treasure hoard visible, detailed scales, fantasy digital art, 8k uhd, trending on artstation"
