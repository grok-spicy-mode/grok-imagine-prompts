# Style Keywords Cheat Sheet

Quick reference for art style and aesthetic keywords in grok imagine prompts.

## Art Movements

| Style | Era | Look | Example Use |
|-------|-----|------|-------------|
| **Impressionist** | 1860s-1880s | Loose brushwork, light effects | "garden scene, impressionist style" |
| **Renaissance** | 1400s-1600s | Realistic, classical | "portrait, renaissance style" |
| **Baroque** | 1600s-1700s | Dramatic, ornate | "scene, baroque style" |
| **Art Nouveau** | 1890s-1910s | Organic curves, decorative | "poster, art nouveau" |
| **Art Deco** | 1920s-1930s | Geometric, luxury | "interior, art deco style" |
| **Cubist** | 1900s-1920s | Geometric, multiple views | "portrait, cubist style" |
| **Surrealist** | 1920s+ | Dreamlike, bizarre | "landscape, surrealist" |
| **Pop Art** | 1950s-1960s | Bold, commercial | "portrait, pop art style" |
| **Minimalist** | 1960s+ | Simple, clean | "design, minimalist" |
